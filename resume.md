- [ECE SkillTree](https://github.com/AzulRadio/ECE-SkillTree-gui) (电气/计算机技能树)

- [D&D Telegram chatbot](https://github.com/AzulRadio/DND-Chatbot)(龙与地下城聊天机器人)
